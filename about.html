About Us

Bark Street Boutique is a premier pet store that was established in 2023 with the aim of providing high-quality pet products and services to pet owners in the local community.

Our goal is to create a one-stop shop for all your pet's needs, offering a wide range of pet supplies, including food, toys, accessories, and grooming services.

At Bark Street Boutique, we are proud to be a community led business. Our passionate and knowledgeable staff are dedicated to providing exceptional customer service and personalized care for every pet that walks through our doors.

We carry a variety of pets, including dogs, cats, birds, small animals, and fish. Whether you're a new pet owner or an experienced one, we have everything you need to keep your furry friends happy and healthy.
